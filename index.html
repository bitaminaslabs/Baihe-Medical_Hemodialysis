The Exponential Advantage

In a fixed catheter shaft, internal space is the primary constraint. Standard round lumens create "dead space." Our D-Shaped design maximizes the internal radius \( r \).

\[ Q \propto r^4 \]

A 10% increase in lumen radius yields a ~46% increase in flow capacity, ensuring superior Kt/V dose delivery.

Advanced Tip Engineering

Split-Tip Dynamics.

Zero Recirculation Strategy

By physically separating the arterial uptake and venous return ports into two distinct "arms", the Split-Tip configuration ensures that filtered blood is returned as far as possible from the arterial intake.

  • Near-zero recirculation rates for peak Kt/V.
  • Independent movement of lumens reduces wall adherence risk.
Tip Architecture

Symmetrical Reciprocity.

Staggered Balance

The Symmetrical Tip design utilizes separated and staggered openings to maintain "Balanced Reciprocity". Unlike traditional step-tips, these are engineered to provide identical hemodynamic performance.

  • Efficient flow even during line reversal.
  • Staggered levels reduce thrombus formation.
  • Consistent performance @ 600+ mL/min.
